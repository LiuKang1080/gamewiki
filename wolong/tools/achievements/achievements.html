Description

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty has a total of: 51 achievements / trophies split into 38 Bronze, 10 Silver, 2 Gold, and 1 Platinum trophy. All achievements can be obtained on New Game. Below is a table containing all of the achievements.

Base Game Achievements

Achievement Description
Wo Long
[Platinum Trophy] You have unlocked all Achievements!
Best Flag Forward
Raise your first Battle Flag.
Sharp Reflexes
Successfully deflect 10 critical blows.
Wizardry Beginner
Learn your first Wizardry Spell. You can view all Wizardry Spells here.
Journey's Beginning
Awaken from the sleep of death at the sound of the Blindfolded Boy's voice. Complete the main battlefield: Village of Calamity.
Righteous Judgment
Exact vengeance 10 times. Vengeance occurs when you kill the same enemy that killed you, recovering your Morale and Qi. You can also enact vengeance on enemies who have killed other players. You can find purple graves where another player has died, kill the enemy that killed the player and vengeance will be enacted.
Vow of the Stalwart
Swear an oath with Zhao Yun. Complete the main battlefield: Two Chivalrous Heros.
Vulnerable Moments
Land 50 Fatal Strikes.
At Fifteen I Joined the Army
Reached Level 15.
Unstoppable
Raise Morale Rank to 20 for the first time. Achievement can be obtained in the main battlefield: Two Chivalrous Heros.
Craftsmanship
Upgrade your first piece of equipment. Use the "Upgrade" option at the Blacksmith. The Blacksmith can be unlocked after completing the main battlefield: The Demon Fort of the Yellow Heaven, and unlocking Hidden Village of Mt.Tianzhushan.
First Phase
Interact with the Five Phases for the first time. You view information on the Five Phases here.
Will of the Loyal
Swore an oath with Guan Yu and Zhang Fei. Complete the main battlefield: The Valley of Crying Wraiths.
Ambition of the Tiger
Swear an oath with Sun Jian. Complete the main battlefield: The Demon Fort of the Yellow Heaven.
How Cute!
Feed your first Shitieshou. Achievement can be obtained in the main battlefield: The Valley of Crying Wraiths.
Guardian of Peace
Swear an oath with Zuo Ci. Complete the main battlefield: In Search of the Immortal Wizard.
Awakening of the Unscrupulous Hero
Swear an oath with Cao Cao. Complete the main battlefield: Fall of the Corrupted Eunuch.
