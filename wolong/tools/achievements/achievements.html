Description

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty has a total of: 51 achievements / trophies split into 38 Bronze, 10 Silver, 2 Gold, and 1 Platinum trophy. All achievements can be obtained on New Game. Below is a table containing all of the achievements.

Base Game Achievements

Achievement Description
Wo Long
[Platinum Trophy] You have unlocked all Achievements!
Best Flag Forward
Raise your first Battle Flag.
Sharp Reflexes
Successfully deflect 10 critical blows.
Wizardry Beginner
Learn your first Wizardry Spell. You can view all Wizardry Spells here.
Journey's Beginning
Awaken from the sleep of death at the sound of the Blindfolded Boy's voice. Complete the main battlefield: Village of Calamity.
Righteous Judgment
Exact vengeance 10 times. Vengeance occurs when you kill the same enemy that killed you, recovering your Morale and Qi. You can also enact vengeance on enemies who have killed other players. You can find purple graves where another player has died, kill the enemy that killed the player and vengeance will be enacted.
Vow of the Stalwart
Swear an oath with Zhao Yun. Complete the main battlefield: Two Chivalrous Heros.
Unstoppable
Raise Morale Rank to 20 for the first time. Achievement can be obtained in the main battlefield: Two Chivalrous Heros.
Vulnerable Moments
Land 50 Fatal Strikes.
At Fifteen I Joined the Army
Reached level 15.
Only at Eighty Did I Finally Return
Reached level 80.
Craftsmanship
Upgrade your first piece of equipment. Use the "Upgrade" option at the Blacksmith. The Blacksmith can be unlocked after completing the main battlefield: The Demon Fort of the Yellow Heaven, and unlocking Hidden Village of Mt.Tianzhushan.
First Phase
Interact with the Five Phases for the first time. You view information on the Five Phases here.
How Cute!
Feed your first Shitieshou. Achievement can be obtained in the main battlefield: The Valley of Crying Wraiths.
Staunching Heresy
Defeat all Demonized Warlocks performing the ritual in the main battlefield: The Valley of Crying Wraiths.
Will of the Loyal
Swore an oath with Guan Yu and Zhang Fei. Complete the main battlefield: The Valley of Crying Wraiths.
Ambition of the Tiger
Swear an oath with Sun Jian. Complete the main battlefield: The Demon Fort of the Yellow Heaven.
Guardian of Peace
Swear an oath with Zuo Ci. Complete the main battlefield: In Search of the Immortal Wizard.
Dogged Justice
Save the captured Hermits in the main battlefield: Fall of the Corrupted Eunuch.
Awakening of the Unscrupulous Hero
Swear an oath with Cao Cao. Complete the main battlefield: Fall of the Corrupted Eunuch.
Strung Bow
Defeat 5 foes with a ballista in the main battlefield: The Battle of Hulaoguan Pass.
Mightiest of Men
Repel Lu Bu's forces. Defeat Lu Bu, and complete the main battlefield: The Battle of Hulaoguan Pass.
Heroes Will Rise
Repel Xielong in the main battlefield: Centuries of Glory Burned Away.
Guidance of the Constellations
Swear an oath with Lady Zhen. Complete the main battlefield: Centuries of Glory Burned Away.
Succession
Swear an oath with Sun Ce, and Sun Quan. Complete the main battlefield: Darkness Over the Hanshui River.
All That Glitters
Embed your first jewel at the Forge. You can embed weapons, armor, and accessories by going to the Blacksmith ⟶ Embedment ⟶ Choosing an Item ⟶ Choose an Embedding Slot ⟶ Embed new attribute.
Side by Side
Dominate any 10 battlefields with a companion. You can summon a companion by resting at a Battle Flag ⟶ Reinforcements ⟶ Choose a companion.
Familial Ties
Swear an oath with Hong Jing. Complete the main battlefield: Tyrant's Final Banquet.
Merit beyond Measure
Receive a sworn brothers' boon for the first time. Reach Oath lvl. 10 (Sworn Brother) with an allied NPC.
The Steadfast Duo
Swear an oath with Xiahou Dun and Xiahou Yuan. Complete the main battlefield: In War's Flames Blaze Fiercely.
Fantastic Form
Equip a full 4★ set consisting of: melee weapon, ranged weapon, accessory, and 4 armor pieces.
Fearless Warrior
Swear an oath with Zhang Liao. Complete the main battlefield: The Way of the Warrior.
Entrusted Legacy
Swear an oath with Lu Bu. Complete the main battlefield: Lu Bu, Mightiest Among Men.
Aspiration and Amity
Swear an oath with Liu Bei. Complete the main battlefield: Glaive of the Righteousness.
Decor Maketh the Man
Decorate your first item at the Forge. Blacksmith ⟶ Decorate ⟶ Choose an Item to Decorate.
Call to Arms
Use 10 Tiger Seals. Rest at a Battle Flag ⟶ Reinforcements ⟶ Choose an NPC warrior to summon.
Eye for an Eye
Fend off invaders 10 times. While going through main and sub battlefield levels red NPCs will invade you, defeat them 10 times.
Dreams of Unity
Swear an oath with Yuan Shao. Complete the main battlefield: The Decisive Battle of Guandu.
Annihilator of Evil
Defeat Yu Ji. Complete the main battlefield: The Decisive Battle of Guandu.
Open Eyes
Hear the Blindfolded Boy's decision. Complete the main battlefield: The Crouching Dragon Roars.
Great Gatherings
Join forces with all available warriors. There are 17 warriors:

1: Cao Cao 2: Cheng Pu 3: Guan Yu 4: Han Dang 5: Hong Jing 6: Huang Gai 7: Liu Bei 8: Sun Ce 9: Sun Jian 10: Sun Quan 11: Xiahou Dun 12: Xiahou Yuan 13: Xun Yu 14: Zhang Fei 15: Zhang Jiao 16: Zhang Liao 17: Zhao Yun
Keen-edged
Upgrade a weapon to its fullest capacity. Reach +9 on any weapon. Blacksmith ⟶ Upgrade. Steel comes in Ranks 1 - 9, and is required to upgrade weapons.
Adamantine
Upgrade a piece of armor to its fullest capacity. Reach +9 on any armor. Blacksmith ⟶ Upgrade. Leather comes in Ranks 1 - 9, and is required to upgrade armor pieces.
Battle-hardened
Dominate 15 sub battlefields.
Wizardry Master
Learn all Wizardry Spells. You can view all Wizardry Spells here.
Hunting the Great Deer
Dominate all main and sub battlefields. Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty contains 46 total battlefields: 16 main battlefields, and 30 sub battlefields.
Fly It High
Raise all Battle Flags. Note: Marking Flags are not required for this achievement.
Pots and Potions
Upgrade the Dragon's Cure Pot to its greatest capacity and effectiveness. Find all Dragon Vein Crystals and Dragon Vein Essences.
Ascension
Give The Hermit of Tianzhu the maximum amount of Golden Cicada Shells.
Well-read
Collect all Tablets.
How Precious!
Feed all of the Shitieshou.
