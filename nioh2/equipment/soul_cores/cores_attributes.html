How to read Conflict Tables

The following information is grouped into 9 tables. Stats within a single table conflict each other, except for the last table. All attributes in the last table do not conflict with each other, nor do they conflict with the other attributes in the other 8 tables. Ex) Looking at the 8th table (Power Stats), both Ninjutsu Power and Onmyo Magic Power are in the same table. A Soul Core that already has Ninjutsu Power cannot roll the Onmyo Magic Power attribute. They conflict.

Anima Bonus (Scorched Enemy) vs. Anima Bonus (Enemy Scorched):

Scorched Enemy = Continuous bonus when dealing damage vs. enemy with that status.
Enemy Scorched = One time bonus on status proc.
