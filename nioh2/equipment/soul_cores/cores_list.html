Description

There are a total of 78 Soul Cores in Nioh 2. 51 Soul Cores are in the base game, 6 Soul Cores are in The Tengu's Disciple (DLC 1), 9 Soul Cores are in The Darkness in the Capital (DLC 2), and 12 Soul Cores are in The First Samurai (DLC 3).

Each Soul Core is 1 of 3 Yokai Forms. The tables below show the default color type of Soul Cores before Mortal Soul Cores are applied:

  • Soul Cores that are colored RED are of the Brute Yokai Form.
  • Soul Cores that are colored PURPLE are of the Phantom Yokai Form.
  • Soul Cores that are colored BLUE are of the Feral Yokai Form.

Below is a full list of all of the Soul Cores present in Nioh 2. You can click on an individual Soul Core to see more information about the enemy that drop that Soul Core.
