Description

There are a total of 37 Guardian Spirits. There are 11 Spirits of the Brute type . 14 Spirits of the Phantom type . And 12 Spirits of the Feral type . Below is a list of all of the Guardian Spirits and their attributes. You can click on an individual Guardian Spirit to see more detail about that spirit.

Legend:
CON: Constitution
HRT: Heart
CRG: Courage
STM: Stamina
STR: Strength
SKL: Skill
DEX: Dexterity
MAG: Magic
: Buff Effect
: Debuff Effect
: Applied effect as Secondary Guardian Spirit (effect is halved)
