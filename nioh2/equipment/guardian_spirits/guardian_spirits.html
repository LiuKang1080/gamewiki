Description

There are a total of 37 Guardian Spirits. There are 11 Spirits of the Brute type . 14 Spirits of the Phantom type . And 12 Spirits of the Feral type .

The Tengu's Disciple (DLC 1) contains 3 Guardian Spirits: Kurama Tengu, Ho, and Blue Dragon.
Darkness In The Capital (DLC 2) contains 4 Guardian Spirits: Kuzunoha The Fox, Oh, Ho-Oh, and Janomecho.
The First Samurai (DLC 3) contains 4 Guardian Spirits: Sohaya, Baku, Suzaku, and Amabie.
All other Guardian Spirits can be obtained in the base game.

The player selects their first Guardian Spirit during the tutorial, after Character Creation. The player recieves their second Guardian Spirit after completing the Main Mission: The Hollow Fortress. When a player switches over to a Guardian Spirit during gameplay that Spirit becomes the Primary Guardian Spirit, and the other Guardian Spirit becomes the Secondary Guardian Spirit. Below is a list of all of the Guardian Spirits and their attributes. You can click on an individual Guardian Spirit to see more details about that spirit.

Guardian Spirit Combination List

Combining different spirits results in an extra special effect, this effect is based on the spirit pairing. All Guardian Spirits are broken up into Element type + Yokai type, EX) Makami is a Fire type + Brute type. There are 5 Elements: Fire, Water, Lightning, Purity, and Corruption. There are 3 Yokai Forms: Brute, Phantom, and Feral. A general formula is followed to get the special effect. Below is a table containing the combinations of Guardian Spirits and the special effect.
