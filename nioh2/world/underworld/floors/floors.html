Layout of each Floor of the Underworld

Each Strata or the Floor of the Underworld is comprised of 2 sections: 1) The Netherscape which contains various enemies and 4 Jizo-Kodama; 2) The Crucible which contains the main boss of the Floor. Once spawned into the Netherscape the player can collect anywhere between 1 and 4 Jizo-Kodama. The player must collect at least 1 Jizo Kodama in order to move onto the Crucible. Once the player has collected at least 1 Jizo-Kodama a portal will appear at the location of where the player collected the Jizo-Kodama as well as a portal next to the Shrine. Once the player is in the Crucible they cannot return back to the Netherscape. In order to go the next Floor of the Underworld the player must defeat the Boss in the Crucible. Below contains the full list of all of the Bosses on each Floor.

Farming For Graces

Specific Graces only drop on specific Floors of the Underworld. All 18 Ethereal Graces are split into 3 Grace Pools (6 Graces in each pool). Only those certain Graces from that Grace pool will be attached to the weapons, armors, and accessories that drop on that specific Floor. The tables below show which Graces drop on which floor. You can view the full details of all of the Graces here. Note Grace of Oyamatsumi and Grace of Shinatsuhiko can only drop from Floors 6 through 30 in The Depths of the Underworld.
