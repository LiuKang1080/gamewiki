Other Notes:
==========
    - Twilight Missions in this difficulty are automatically unlocked when the region their main mission is in are unlocked.
    - All missions in the Awakening Region (region 1) are automatically unlocked after beating the mission In the Eye of the
        Beholder in Dream of the Samurai difficulty.
    - All missions in the Soaring Region (region 2) are automatically unlocked after beating the mission In the Eye of the
        Beholder in Dream of the Samurai difficulty.
    - The Shadow Region (region 3) is fully unlocked after completing 4 missions.
    - The Dawn Region (region 4) and the Twilight Region (region 5) are fully unlocked after completing 8 missions.
    - The following Dojo Missions are unlocked after completing 8 missions:
        - The Way of the Warrior: Veteran
        - The Way of the Ninja: Veteran
        - The Way of Onmyo: Veteran
        - Opening of the Mind's Eye
        - Master of the Dual Blades
        - Hanzo the Demon
        - The Unmoving Mountain
        - The Chain That Splits the Sky
        - The Tengu's Blade
        - Like Lightning
        - The Unstoppable Whirlwind
        - Sharp Eyes
        - The One Who Lives in the Shadows
    - The Dream Region (region 6) is fully unlocked after completing 12 missions.
    - The Dojo Mission: The Nine Symbols is unlocked after completing 12 missions.
    - The following regions are unlocked after completing 18 missions:
        - Afterglow Region (region 7)
        - The Tengu's Disciple (DLC 1)
        - Darkness In The Capital (DLC 2)
        - The First Samurai (DLC 3)
    - The following Dojo Missions are unlocked after completing 18 missions:
        - Breaking Free
        - Strike Like a Yokai
