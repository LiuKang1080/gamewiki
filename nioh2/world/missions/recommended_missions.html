Recommended Missions To Reach Next Difficulty

  1. Awakening Region (1st Region)
  2. Soaring Region (2nd Region)
  3. Shadow Region (3rd Region)
  4. Dawn Region (4th Region)
  5. Twilight Region (5th Region)
  6. Dream Region (6th Region)
  7. Afterglow Region (7th Region)
  8. Credits

Description

The player must complete at least 18 missions to unlock the mission In the Eye of the Beholder in the final region (Afterglow). Below is a recommended list of missions to complete, seperated by each region. Completing In the Eye of the Beholder unlocks the next difficulty, and is required. We want to select short and fast missions. Generally these missions will be gauntlet style / linear missions. If we can acquire Locks of Hair, or other very important items during these short missions then that would be optimal, but we will generally not go out of our way to get these items. We can always come back later and acquire the Locks of Hair, or the other important items.

Awakening Region (1st Region):

It is generally recommended that we complete the entire first region. This will give us the opportunity to soul match and level up so that we meet the damage output required for the difficulty.

The Village of the Cursed Blossoms The Forest Veiled in Darkness A Voice in the Twlight The Search The Greedy Hunter The Beast Born of Smoke and Flames (Potentially Skip) A Favor for the Blacksmith Dark Omens The Viper's Sanctum (Potentially Skip)

Soaring Region (2nd Region):

An End to Seclusion Hidetatsu's Dying Wish The Viper and the Butterfly (Potentially Skip) An Error in Calculation The Fire God's Magatama (Potentially Skip) A Hot-Blooded Howl (Potentially Skip)

Shadow Region (3rd Region):

The Golden Nation Learning a Lession Lingering Snow and Crimson Tracks (Potentially Skip) Immovable (Potentially Skip) The Shrine of Oaths The Fallen Star (Potentially Skip) The Tiger Approaches

Dawn Region (4th Region):

The Aberrant Mage Abduction The Warrior The Burning Sky (Potentially Skip) The Missing Gun (Potentially Skip)

Twilight Region (5th Region):

Stray Cats A Message From the Deceased Scattered Fragments Calling on the Fire God

Dream Region (6th Region):

The Bravest of the Brave The Roaming Artisan

Afterglow Region (7th Region):

Before we face the final boss Otakemaru in the mission In the Eye of the Beholder, we can level up and soul match our weapons a final time by playing the mission Dawn of Hope. The gauntlet section with the Magatsu Warrior can be challenging, especially on the higher difficulties. Use William's AI! Reminder that we're here to farm materials, level up, and soul match, we do not need to complete this mission fully.

Dawn of Hope (Potentially Skip) In the Eye of the Beholder (!!! REQUIRED to move to the next difficulty !!!)
