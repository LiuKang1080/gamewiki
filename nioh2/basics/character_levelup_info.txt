========== ========== ==========
Samurai Skill Points
========== ========== ==========
    - There are a total of 20 Samurai Skill Points that can be obtained from leveling up.
    - Gain +1 Samurai Skill Point upon reaching level:
    3, 5, 7, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 100, 110, 120, and 130
